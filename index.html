My Two Year Goals

Year One

React: Build complex applications with React, diving deeper into hooks, context API, and performance optimization techniques.

Version Control and Collaboration

Git & GitHub: Master advanced Git techniques, including branching strategies, pull requests, and collaborative workflows. Contribute to open-source projects to gain practical experience.

Build a Strong Portfolio

Develop a portfolio of diverse projects showcasing various skills, including responsive design, interactive elements, and integration with APIs. Ensure projects are well-documented and deployed.

Achieving Career Milestones

Aim to secure a mid-level front-end developer position, leveraging enhanced skills and a strong portfolio. Continuously seek opportunities for professional growth and advancement within the industry.

Continuous Professional Development

Stay updated with the latest trends, tools, and best practices in front-end development by following industry blogs, attending webinars, and participating in tech conferences and meetups.

Complete advanced certifications (e.g., freeCodeCamp, Coursera, Udacity) to validate skills and gain in-depth knowledge in specific areas of interest.

Performance Optimization and Testing

Optimize web performance by learning techniques such as lazy loading, code splitting, and caching. Gain proficiency in testing frameworks like Jest and Cypress to ensure robust and reliable code.

UI/UX Design Principles

Study and apply UI/UX design principles to create user-friendly and visually appealing interfaces. Learn design tools like Figma or Sketch to create wireframes and prototypes.
